Tutorial Wordle

v1.0

How To Play

Guess the tutorial term in 6 tries.

  • Each guess must be a valid word of the correct length.
  • The colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was.

Enter the target word for this session. Students will try to guess it. Words can be 3 to 14 letters long.
